Our Programs — Kuchipudi Curriculum

Courses for all ages & levels

Beginners

  • Basic stance (Aramandi)
  • Fundamental Adavus training
  • Introduction to Abhinaya (expressions)
  • Basic Tala & rhythm practice
  • Ideal for fresh learners of all ages

Intermediate

  • Adavu combinations & Nritya sequences
  • Footwork + rhythm refinement
  • Enhanced Abhinaya skills
  • Prep for group stage performance

Advanced

  • Full repertoire & Varnams
  • Deep character portrayal
  • Stage polishing & stamina building
  • Advanced rhythmic play & creativity
  • Training for professional artists

Why to Learn from Guru Shri. V Rama Raju at Mayuri

Authentic — Personal — Structured

Authentic Technique

We preserve the purity of Kuchipudi through rigorous training rooted in the traditional Guru Shishya parampara, ensuring every movement, expression, and rhythm aligns with classical standards passed down for generations.

Personal Guidance

With small personalized batches, every learner receives dedicated correction, mentorship and detailed attention—helping them grow with confidence and grace at their own pace.

Flexible & Global Learning

We offer online and offline batches with convenient timing options for students across India and abroad, enabling passionate learners to continue their training from anywhere in the world.

Performance & Stage Exposure

Students gain opportunities to perform at national & international festivals, workshops, annual showcases and certification exams, building real-stage confidence and professional readiness.
