Preserving Kuchipudi for 35+ Years

Mayuri Kuchipudi Academy has been a pillar of Kuchipudi excellence for over three decades, shaping dancers across Andhra Pradesh and around the world. With deep cultural roots and an unwavering dedication to classical art, we continue to pass on the true essence of Kuchipudi to the next generation.

With 50+ prestigious awards and thousands of successful students, our academy now brings this traditional learning experience directly to your home through online classes.

  • Guru with 35+ Years of Experience
  • 50+ Awards Across India
  • Now Available Online — Learn From Home

About Mayuri

Our academy believes in disciplined practice, classical grounding, and spiritual connection to dance. Every step, every expression, and every movement is taught with care and attention to detail.

Whether you're a beginner looking to start your classical journey or an experienced dancer aiming to refine your craft, our structured online format ensures you receive personalized, guru-led training from anywhere in the world.

Shri V. Rama Raju – Artistic Director

  • Shri V. Rama Raju, our Artistic Director was trained at the Siddhendra Yogi Kuchipudi Kala Peetham, the historical cradle of the Kuchipudi art form and a major centre for learning Indian performing arts.
  • Shri V. Rama Raju is a professional classical dancer under the tutelage of the legendary Padma Bhushan Dr. Vempati Chinna Satyam, the maestro credited with refining the art form and bringing it to the global stage.
  • Shri V. Rama Raju embodies immense & rich experience in performing art, teaching and choreography and is also a firm believer in spreading this knowledge and skill to the best of his ability.
